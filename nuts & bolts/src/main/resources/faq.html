Some questions answered regarding Knots and Bolts online shopping

Question: How do I contact the support?

Answer: We have provided the contact number, Fax, Email and physical address. If in case you want to contact "Knots and Bolts" use any of the provided method
Question: How can I return the items that I purchased?

Answer: To return the purchases items, follow the instructions below:
1, Go to your Order
2.Click the item you want to return
3.When you get redirected to the next page,click view order details
4. Click return the item
Question: How do I edit the cart?

Answer: Editing the cart is simple, just click the cart on right hand site,that will redirect to a page with active shopping card,click "delete" to remove items, click "+" or "-" for quantities you wish to purchase
